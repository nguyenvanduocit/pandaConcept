## Quick Style Lookup

### Modern & Contemporary
- **Minimalist** тАФ Japanese (zen, ma, tatami) / Scandinavian (hygge, birch, lagom) / Warm (limewash, travertine, boucl├й)
- **Scandinavian** тАФ Danish/Swedish/Norwegian/Finnish. Light woods, white, functional warmth
- **Mid-Century Modern** тАФ 1950s-60s organic forms, teak, walnut, Eames, Saarinen
- **Japandi** тАФ Japanese wabi-sabi + Scandi hygge. Shou sugi ban, washi, live-edge oak
- **Contemporary (2024-25)** тАФ Curved forms, boucl├й, travertine, limewash, warm earth tones
- **Bauhaus** тАФ Tubular steel, primary colors, geometric purity, Wassily/Barcelona chairs

### Classic & Traditional
- **Baroque** (1600-1750) тАФ Gilded grandeur, trompe-l'┼Уil, velvet, crystal chandeliers
- **Colonial** тАФ American (pine, hearth) / British (rattan, mahogany) / Spanish (terracotta, stucco)
- **Victorian** тАФ Early (Gothic Revival) / High (maximalist) / Late (Aesthetic/Eastlake)
- **Neoclassical** (1750-1850) тАФ Greek/Roman symmetry, fluted columns, Wedgwood blue
- **Art Deco** (1920s-30s) тАФ Geometric glamour, Macassar ebony, chrome, black+gold
- **French Provincial** тАФ Rustic elegance, toile, lavender, carved walnut armoire
- **Regency** (1800-1830) тАФ Sabre legs, brass inlay, rosewood, "Bridgerton" aesthetic
- **Georgian** (1714-1830) тАФ Palladian symmetry, Chippendale, mahogany
- **Rococo** (1715-1775) тАФ Pastel asymmetry, boiserie, shell motifs, gilded berg├иre
- **Tudor** (1485-1603) тАФ Exposed oak beams, inglenook fireplace, linenfold paneling

### Asian & Eastern
- **Japanese (Wabi-Sabi)** тАФ Tatami, shoji, tokonoma, hinoki cypress
- **Chinese Traditional** тАФ Ming (huanghuali) / Qing (ornate lacquer, red+gold)
- **Vietnamese** тАФ Tropical bamboo+rattan, French-colonial blend
- **Indochine** тАФ French-Asian fusion, dark wood, rattan, silk
- **Korean (Hanok)** тАФ Ondol floors, hanji paper, pine, celadon
- **Thai** тАФ Teak, gilded carving, naga motifs, Thai silk
- **Balinese** тАФ Open-air bale pavilions, paras stone, alang-alang thatch
- **Moroccan/Moorish** тАФ Zellige tile, tadelakt, riad courtyard
- **Indian** тАФ Mughal (marble, jali screens) / Rajasthani (mirror work, vibrant color)
- **Persian** тАФ Persian carpets, muqarnas, turquoise+lapis, iwan courtyards

### Regional & Vernacular
- **Mediterranean** тАФ Greek (white+blue) / Italian (terracotta, Murano) / Spanish (azulejo)
- **Tropical** тАФ SE Asian / Caribbean / Hawaiian. Rattan, bamboo, open-air
- **Coastal/Hamptons** тАФ Shiplap, navy+white, linen, sisal
- **Rustic** тАФ American lodge / European / Mountain. Log, stone, cowhide
- **Farmhouse** тАФ Traditional / Modern. Shiplap, apron sink, barn doors
- **Desert/Southwestern** тАФ Adobe, vigas, saltillo, kiva fireplace, turquoise

### Specialty & Avant-Garde
- **Industrial** тАФ NYC Loft / Warehouse / Soft Industrial. Brick, concrete, matte black
- **Brutalist** тАФ Raw concrete, board-formed, geometric mass, Corten steel
- **Biophilic** тАФ Living walls, natural light, organic forms, wellness
- **Maximalist** тАФ Curated / Eclectic. Jewel tones, velvet, pattern mixing
- **Retro** тАФ 50s (pastel) / 60s (space-age) / 70s (earth, shag) / 80s (neon, Memphis)
- **Futuristic** тАФ Parametric, LED, smart glass, carbon fiber
- **Bohemian** тАФ Traditional / Modern / Chic. Macram├й, kilim, rattan
- **Wabi-Kintsugi** тАФ Gold-repaired ceramics, weathered materials, imperfection beauty

## Gotchas

- **Sub-styles matter**: "Mediterranean" тЖТ specify Greek/Italian/Spanish. "Victorian" тЖТ Early/High/Late.
- **Keywords are provider-sensitive**: See Provider-Specific Notes in `reference.md`.
- **Cultural sensitivity**: Asian styles have sacred elements тАФ check Cultural Notes before rendering.
- **Styles blend**: "Modern but warm" = Japandi or Warm Minimalism, not strict Modern.
- **Hex codes are starting points**: Let design brief take priority over canonical colors.
